SakuraLLM Attribution
=====================

On-device Japanese-to-Chinese translation in this app is powered by
SakuraLLM (https://github.com/SakuraLLM/SakuraLLM), specifically the
Sakura-1.5B Qwen2.5 GGUF quantization.

The Sakura model is fine-tuned from Qwen2.5-1.5B and is distributed
under the original SakuraLLM repository license. The underlying base
model Qwen2.5 is licensed under the Tongyi Qianwen LICENSE AGREEMENT
(https://huggingface.co/Qwen/Qwen2.5-1.5B/blob/main/LICENSE).

This product is not affiliated with or endorsed by the SakuraLLM
project or Alibaba / Tongyi team. The model weights are downloaded
on demand from official mirrors at runtime; they are not embedded in
the APK.
