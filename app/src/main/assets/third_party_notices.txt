Google ML Kit
=============

The application uses Google ML Kit Text Recognition 16.0.1 for Latin, Chinese,
Japanese and Korean text, and ML Kit Translation 17.0.3 with language models
downloaded on demand. ML Kit is a Google SDK governed by the ML Kit Terms and
is not represented here as open-source software.

Input images and text are processed on-device and are not sent to Google. The
SDK may contact Google for model or compatibility updates and may send
performance and utilization metrics that do not contain the input or output.
This data-processing disclosure is also shown in the ML Kit settings. Terms and
privacy details:
https://developers.google.com/ml-kit/terms
